Instructions for Use

  1. Wear the watch on your non-dominant wrist
  2. Ensure good skin contact (tight but comfortable fit)
  3. Turn on the device and open the mobile app
  4. Check that Bluetooth is enabled and pairing is active
  5. Allow the device to record continuously during daily activity
  6. If notified of irregular rhythm, rest and confirm via app
  7. Review your history in the app weekly
  8. Export data if instructed by your healthcare provider
  9. Keep firmware updated via app when prompted
  10. Recharge daily for optimal data collection
